Random Android Facts

Android is based on a modified version of the Linux kernel.

Android is developed by the Open Handset Alliance and commercially sponsored by Google.

The unofficial Android-x86 project provided support for x86 architectures ahead of the official support.

Although the mascot has no official name, the Android team reportedly call it "Bugdroid".
